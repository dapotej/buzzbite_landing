About Buzzbite

We started Buzzbite because the problem was obvious.

Brands wanted to reach diverse audiences. Diverse creators wanted access to brand budgets. The tools to connect them intelligently didn't exist. So we built them.

Our Story

Built from frustration.

Working in influencer marketing, we kept seeing the same pattern. Brands would tell us they wanted more diverse creator partnerships. We'd look at the tools available and find demographic filters buried in platforms built for a completely different use case.

The result was predictable — rushed decisions, poorly matched creators, campaigns that felt performative rather than authentic. And after it all, no way to prove whether it worked.

Buzzbite exists to change that. Not by adding another filter to an existing platform — but by building cultural intelligence into the foundation of how creator discovery works.

What we believe

Authenticity beats optics

A campaign that genuinely resonates with a community is worth more than ten that tick a box.

Data should serve people

Technology is only useful if it helps real creators get fair access to real budgets.

Diversity is business-critical

Reaching diverse audiences isn't charity. It's how smart brands grow.

Tech scales, agencies don't

We're building a platform — not just a services business — because impact requires scale.
