The Challenge

Most brands don't know
where their diversity gaps are.

The problem isn't intent. It's infrastructure. Without the right data and framework, diversity in creator marketing stays performative.

📋

No baseline data

Without auditing existing spend, brands can't know which communities are underrepresented in their creator mix — or by how much. You can't improve what you haven't measured.

🎯

Discovery tools exclude the wrong creators

Mainstream platforms surface creators by follower count and historical performance. Diverse nano and micro-creators — who consistently outperform on engagement and purchase intent — are structurally invisible.

💸

Pay gaps are baked into standard pricing

Diverse creators are systematically undercompensated because pricing reflects historical market rates — which already encoded inequality. Without equity-aware pricing guidance, brands perpetuate the problem even when they're trying to fix it.

📣

Strategy without execution is just a document

DEI marketing audits that don't connect to operational workflow, creator discovery tools, and revenue measurement don't change anything. Strategy has to be wired to how campaigns actually run.
