The Challenge

Most brands don't know where
their diversity gaps are.

📋

No baseline data

Without auditing existing spend, brands can't know which communities are underrepresented in their creator mix — or by how much.

🎯

Unclear priorities

With dozens of communities and limited budget, brands struggle to decide where diversity investment will have the most impact.

📣

Stakeholder alignment

DEI boards, CMOs, and marketing teams often have different definitions of success. Without a shared framework, progress stalls.

⚖️

Risk of getting it wrong

Poorly planned diversity campaigns cause more damage than no campaign at all. Strategy reduces risk by grounding decisions in data.
