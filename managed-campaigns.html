The Process

Brief to live in as little as 2 weeks.

A structured workflow that takes you from campaign objective to revenue data — without the spreadsheet chaos.

01

Discovery & Brief

We start with a strategy call to understand your brand, DTC goals, target communities, and budget. We produce a detailed creator brief and campaign plan — including whether gifting, paid, or a combined approach is right for this objective.

02

Creator Matching

Our team runs your brief through the Buzzbite discovery engine, surfacing nano and micro-creators scored on audience alignment, cultural relevance, and engagement quality — not follower counts. You review and approve the final roster before we proceed.

03

Gifting & Seeding (if applicable)

For campaigns that start with product seeding, we handle product selection confirmation, address collection, shipment tracking, and content monitoring. Top performers from gifting are flagged for the paid phase — closing the loop from seeding to revenue attribution.

04

Outreach, Briefs & Approvals

We handle all creator outreach, negotiation, and contracts. Our team briefs creators, manages revision rounds, and signs off on content against your brand guidelines — with full visibility in the platform at every stage.

05

Revenue Reporting

Post-campaign you receive a full performance report tied to CAC, ROAS, and LTV — not just reach and impressions. You'll know which creators moved the needle and which to double down on in the next campaign. Ready to share with leadership.
