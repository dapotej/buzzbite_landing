The Process

From brief to live in
as little as 2 weeks.

01

Discovery & Brief

We start with a 60-minute strategy call to understand your brand, campaign objectives, target communities, and budget. We then produce a detailed creator brief and campaign plan for your approval.

02

Creator Matching

Our team runs your brief through the Buzzbite platform, shortlisting creators scored for cultural fit — not just demographics. You review and approve a final creator roster before we proceed.

03

Outreach & Contracts

We handle all creator outreach, negotiation, and contract management. Our established relationships across the creator network mean faster responses and fairer rates.

04

Content & Delivery

We brief creators, review content drafts, manage revision rounds, and sign off on final posts — ensuring every piece of content meets your brand guidelines and campaign goals.

05

Reporting & Insights

Post-campaign, you receive a full performance report including reach, engagement, Cultural Fit Score outcomes, community sentiment analysis, and ROI — ready to share with leadership.
