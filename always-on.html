How It Works

Your always-on engine.

Three phases that run continuously — seeding, activating, and evolving the creator roster month by month.

Month 1–2

Build Your Roster

We identify and onboard a curated group of nano and micro-creators — the right cultural fit for your brand, your target communities, and your markets. Gifting campaigns seed your product to nano-creators and begin collecting performance signals.

→
Ongoing Monthly

Activate & Measure

Each month, we brief active creators, manage content approvals, publish to your content calendar, and track revenue attribution. Top-performing nano-creators are graduated into paid micro-creator briefs. You get a live status board and monthly revenue report.

→
Quarterly

Review & Evolve

Quarterly reviews assess CAC, ROAS, and LTV by creator. We refresh the roster with new talent from the gifting pipeline, realign strategy based on what's performing, and set objectives for the next quarter. The programme gets smarter with every cycle.
